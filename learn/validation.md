# Validation Stage

You have an idea and you've stress-tested it. Now talk to real people and find signal before you build.

---

## Books

### [The Mom Test](https://www.amazon.in/Mom-Test-customers-business-everyone/dp/1492180742)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** Rob Fitzpatrick  
**Why it helps:** The definitive guide to customer conversations. Teaches you to ask questions that reveal truth, not comfort.

### [Talking to Humans](https://www.talkingtohumans.com/)
**Type:** Book (free PDF)  
**Author:** Giff Constable  
**Why it helps:** A short, practical guide to qualitative customer research. Free to download.

### [Sprint](https://www.amazon.in/Sprint-Solve-Problems-Test-Ideas/dp/150112174X)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** Jake Knapp  
**Why it helps:** Google Ventures' 5-day process to prototype and test ideas with real users before committing to building.

**Type:** Video  
**Channel:** Y Combinator  
**Why it helps:** Why the best validation often involves doing things manually and personally before automating anything.

### [Finding Product Market Fit — Emmett Shear](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_6pl5GG8RQ4)
**Type:** Video  
**Channel:** Y Combinator  
**Why it helps:** Clear framework for knowing when you have PMF — and honest about how rare it is.

## Case Studies

### [How Airbnb validated with 3 air mattresses](https://techcrunch.com/2012/08/01/airbnbs-brian-chesky-on-the-origins-of-airbnb-and-the-importance-of-focus/)

**Why it helps:** Brian Chesky put up a website for his own apartment before building anything. The first validation was one booking.
