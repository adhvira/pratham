# Scaling Stage

You have early traction. Now grow it without breaking what works.

---

## Books

### [Blitzscaling](https://www.amazon.in/Blitzscaling-Lightning-Fast-Building-Massively-Companies/dp/0008303630)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** Reid Hoffman  
**Why it helps:** When and how to grow fast — and the specific things that break as you scale that you need to plan for.

### [High Output Management](https://www.amazon.in/High-Output-Management-Andrew-Grove/dp/0679762884)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** Andy Grove  
**Why it helps:** How to manage a team as it grows. The most practical management book ever written.

### [Traction](https://www.amazon.in/Traction-Startup-Achieve-Explosive-Customer/dp/1591848369)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** Gabriel Weinberg & Justin Mares  
**Why it helps:** 19 channels for acquiring customers, and a framework for finding which one works for your business.

### [Good to Great](https://www.amazon.in/Good-Great-Some-Companies-Others/dp/0712676090)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** Jim Collins  
**Why it helps:** What separates companies that sustain growth from those that plateau — the Flywheel concept is essential.
