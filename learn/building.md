# Building Stage

You've validated. Now build the smallest thing that works and get it in front of real users.

---

## Books

### [Shape Up](https://basecamp.com/shapeup)
**Type:** Book (free online)  
**Author:** Ryan Singer, Basecamp  
**Why it helps:** How to scope, build, and ship features without sprints or backlogs overwhelming your small team.

### [The Pragmatic Programmer](https://www.amazon.in/Pragmatic-Programmer-journey-mastery-Anniversary/dp/0135957052)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** David Thomas & Andrew Hunt  
**Why it helps:** The fundamentals of writing good software — relevant for any technical founder building their first product.

### [Inspired](https://www.amazon.in/INSPIRED-Create-Tech-Products-Customers/dp/1119387507)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** Marty Cagan  
**Why it helps:** How the best product teams decide what to build — essential for founders who are also product managers.

### [An Elegant Puzzle](https://www.amazon.in/Elegant-Puzzle-Systems-Engineering-Management/dp/1732265186)
**Type:** Book  
**Author:** Will Larson  
**Why it helps:** Engineering management for small teams — how to structure work and make technical decisions at the early stage.
