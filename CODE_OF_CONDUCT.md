# Code of Conduct

Pratham is built by and for first-time Indian founders. We are committed to keeping this a welcoming, inclusive, and honest community.

---

## Our standards

**We expect:**
- Honest, accurate contributions — only add what you can verify
- Respectful engagement with other contributors
- Constructive feedback on PRs and issues
- Transparency when you have a conflict of interest

**We do not allow:**
- Promotional contributions — adding resources to market your own product
- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile language of any kind
- Submitting false or outdated information knowingly
- Spam or low-effort contributions

---

## Enforcement

Contributions that violate this code will be closed without merging. Repeated violations will result in being blocked from the repository.

---

## Reporting

If you see something that violates this code, email **hello@adhvira.com**
