# Institutional Grants

Grants from universities, IITs, IIMs, and research institutions.

---

### IIT Madras Pravartak — Student Innovation Grant
**Source:** IIT Madras  
**Amount:** Up to ₹5L  
**Eligibility:** Students and alumni of IIT Madras. Open to non-IIT founders through Pravartak programs.  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue  
**Deadline:** Annual — check Pravartak website  
**Apply:** [pravartak.org.in](https://pravartak.org.in)  
**Notes:** One of India's most active IIT incubators. Even if you're not from IIT Madras, Pravartak runs open programs.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### IIM Bangalore — NSRCEL Seed Grant
**Source:** NSRCEL, IIM Bangalore  
**Amount:** Up to ₹10L  
**Eligibility:** Startups accepted into NSRCEL incubation program  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Deadline:** Cohort-based  
**Apply:** [nsrcel.org](https://www.nsrcel.org)  
**Notes:** Must be part of the NSRCEL incubation cohort to access seed grants.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### SINE — IIT Bombay Seed Funding
**Source:** SINE, IIT Bombay  
**Amount:** Up to ₹25L  
**Eligibility:** Startups in SINE incubation program  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Deadline:** Rolling — through SINE application  
**Apply:** [sineiitb.org](https://www.sineiitb.org)  
**Notes:** Tech-focused. Strong for deep tech, software, and hardware startups.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### IISc — SID Innovation Grant
**Source:** Society for Innovation and Development, IISc  
**Amount:** Varies — up to ₹50L for deep tech  
**Eligibility:** Deep tech startups, research-backed innovation  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue  
**Deadline:** Rolling  
**Apply:** [sid.iisc.ac.in](https://sid.iisc.ac.in)  
**Notes:** Best for founders with a research background or IP to protect.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

### Villgro Innovation Foundation
**Source:** Villgro  
**Amount:** Up to ₹50L (grant + investment)  
**Eligibility:** Social impact startups in health, agriculture, and education  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue  
**Deadline:** Rolling
