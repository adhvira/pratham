# Government Grants & Schemes

---

### Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)
**Source:** DPIIT / Startup India  
**Amount:** Up to ₹20L (PoC) + up to ₹50L (market entry)  
**Eligibility:** DPIIT-recognised startups, incorporated within 2 years, not received > ₹10L in other government grants  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue  
**Deadline:** Rolling — applied through empanelled incubators  
**Apply:** [seedfund.startupindia.gov.in](https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in)  
**Notes:** Apply through your nearest SISFS-empanelled incubator, not directly. List of empanelled incubators available on the website.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### MUDRA Tarun Loan
**Source:** MUDRA / Ministry of Finance  
**Amount:** ₹5L – ₹10L  
**Eligibility:** Indian citizen, business in manufacturing, trading, or services, no existing MUDRA default  
**Stage:** Any — works for early-stage businesses  
**Deadline:** Rolling  
**Apply:** [mudra.org.in](https://www.mudra.org.in) or via JanSamarth portal  
**Notes:** Apply via JanSamarth for in-principle digital approval before visiting the bank. DPIIT recognition and Udyam registration strengthen your application significantly.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### Atal Innovation Mission — Grants
**Source:** NITI Aayog / AIM  
**Amount:** Varies by program (up to ₹1Cr for selected cohorts)  
**Eligibility:** Students, startups, and innovators. Program-specific eligibility.  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue  
**Deadline:** Program-specific — check AIM website  
**Apply:** [aim.gov.in](https://aim.gov.in)  
**Notes:** Multiple grant programs under AIM — ATL, AIM iCreate, AIM PRIME. Check which program suits your stage.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### BIRAC — Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council
**Source:** Department of Biotechnology, Government of India  
**Amount:** Up to ₹50L (BIG grants) / Up to ₹1.5Cr (LEAP grants)  
**Eligibility:** Indian startups in biotech, health, agri-tech. DPIIT recognised preferred.  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Deadline:** Rolling  
**Apply:** [birac.nic.in](https://birac.nic.in)  
**Notes:** If you're building in health tech, biotech, or agri-tech, BIRAC is one of the best government grant sources available.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### DST NIDHI — Entrepreneur in Residence
**Source:** Department of Science & Technology  
**Amount:** ₹30,000/month stipend for 12 months  
**Eligibility:** Innovators with a technology-based idea, working through a DST-supported incubator  
**Stage:** Pre-idea / Idea  
**Deadline:** Rolling — through DST-supported incubators  
**Apply:** [nidhi.dst.gov.in](https://nidhi.dst.gov.in)  
**Notes:** Designed for people who want to work full-time on their idea but can't afford to quit their job or college stipend. Underutilised and worth exploring.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### T-Fund — Telangana State Government
**Source:** Government of Telangana / T-Hub  
**Amount:** Up to ₹25L  
**Eligibility:** Telangana-based startups, DPIIT recognised  
**Stage:** Early revenue  
**Deadline:** Cohort-based — check T-Hub website  
**Apply:** [thub.org.in](https://thub.org.in)  
**Notes:** Specifically for Hyderabad and Telangana-based founders. Worth pursuing if you're based there.  
**Last verified:** June 2026
