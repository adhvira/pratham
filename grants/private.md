# Private Foundation & Corporate Grants

---

### Tata Social Enterprise Challenge
**Source:** Tata Group / IIM Calcutta  
**Amount:** Up to ₹15L + incubation  
**Eligibility:** Social enterprises addressing India's development challenges. Student founders welcome.  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue  
**Deadline:** Annual — usually opens August, closes October  
**Apply:** [tsec.net](https://tsec.net)  
**Notes:** One of India's most respected social enterprise competitions. Even without winning, the network and visibility are valuable.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### Nasscom Foundation — Tech for Good
**Source:** Nasscom Foundation  
**Amount:** Varies  
**Eligibility:** Tech-for-good startups using technology for social impact  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue  
**Deadline:** Program-specific  
**Apply:** [nasscomfoundation.org](https://nasscomfoundation.org)  
**Notes:** Strong network in the Indian tech industry. Good for founders building in edtech, healthtech, or civic tech.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### Qualcomm Design in India Challenge
**Source:** Qualcomm India  
**Amount:** Up to $100,000 in prizes  
**Eligibility:** Indian startups building hardware / semiconductor / IoT products  
**Stage:** Any  
**Deadline:** Annual — check Qualcomm India website  
**Apply:** [qualcomm.com/research/university-relations/india](https://www.qualcomm.com/research/university-relations/india)  
**Notes:** Hardware and IoT focused. One of the better-funded private challenges for deep tech founders in India.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### Reliance Foundation — JioGenNext
**Source:** Reliance Jio / Reliance Foundation  
**Amount:** Acceleration support + ₹10L seed funding for selected startups  
**Eligibility:** Indian startups, tech-focused  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Deadline:** Cohort-based  
**Apply:** [jiogennext.com](https://www.jiogennext.com)  
**Notes:** Access to Jio's distribution network and enterprise customer base is the real value here — beyond the funding.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### Elevate — Karnataka Government & Private Partners
**Source:** Karnataka Government + private partners  
**Amount:** Up to ₹50L  
**Eligibility:** Karnataka-based startups, any domain  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Deadline:** Annual  
**Apply:** [startup.karnataka.gov.in/elevate](https://startup.karnataka.gov.in)  
**Notes:** One of the largest state-level startup grant programs in India. Highly competitive but worth applying.  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### Shell Springboard India
**Source:** Shell Foundation  
**Amount:** Up to ₹20L + global mentorship  
**Eligibility:** Clean energy and sustainability startups in India  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Deadline:** Annual  
**Apply:** [shell.com/springboard](https://www.shell.com/energy-and-innovation/new-energies/shell-springboard.html)  
**Notes:** Best for founders building in clean energy, climate tech, or sustainability. Shell's global network is the real prize.  
**Last verified:** June 2026
