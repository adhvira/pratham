### SINE — IIT Bombay
**Organisation:** IIT Bombay  
**City:** Mumbai  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Cost:** Free / Equity for investment  
**Duration:** 12–18 months  
**What they offer:** IIT Bombay's incubation cell — one of India's best. Lab access, mentorship from faculty and industry, investor connections, and the IIT Bombay alumni network.  
**Apply:** [sineiitb.org](https://www.sineiitb.org)  
**Deadline:** Rolling  
**Last verified:** June 2026
