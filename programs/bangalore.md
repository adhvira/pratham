# Programs — Bangalore & Karnataka

### NSRCEL — IIM Bangalore
**Organisation:** IIM Bangalore  
**City:** Bangalore  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Cost:** Free (selected cohorts)  
**Duration:** 6–12 months  
**What they offer:** One of India's most respected incubators. Mentorship from IIM faculty and alumni, investor connections, legal and financial support, and a strong alumni network.  
**Apply:** [nsrcel.org](https://www.nsrcel.org)  
**Deadline:** Rolling — cohorts open 2–3 times a year  
**Last verified:** June 2026
