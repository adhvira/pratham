# Programs — Pan-India

Programs open to founders anywhere in India.

---

### Startup India Recognition (DPIIT)
**Organisation:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade  
**City:** Online / Pan-India  
**Stage:** Any  
**Cost:** Free  
**Duration:** Ongoing  
**What they offer:** Official DPIIT recognition as a startup — unlocks tax benefits, fast-track patent filing, government tender eligibility, and access to MUDRA and Startup India Seed Fund.  
**Apply:** [startupindia.gov.in](https://www.startupindia.gov.in)  
**Deadline:** Rolling  
**Last verified:** June 2026

---

### Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)
**Organisation:** DPIIT / Startup India  
**City:** Online / Pan-India  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue  
**Cost:** Free (grant)  
**Duration:** One-time grant  
**What they offer:** Up to ₹20L for proof of concept and ₹50L for market entry. Applied through empanelled incubators.  
**Apply:** [seedfund.startupindia.gov.in](https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in)  
**Deadline:** Rolling — apply through nearest empanelled incubator  
**Last verified:** June 2026

### Google for Startups — Accelerator India
**Organisation:** Google  
**City:** Online / Bangalore  
**Stage:** Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Cost:** Free  
**Duration:** 3 months  
**What they offer:** Google Workspace credits, mentorship from Google teams, technical support (Cloud, AI/ML), and investor introductions. Equity-free.
