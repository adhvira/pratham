# Pratham

> *Sanskrit: प्रथम — First.*

An open source resource hub for first-time Indian founders. Free forever. Built and maintained by the community.

**pratham.adhvira.com** · [Contribute](#contributing) · [Report an issue](https://github.com/adhvira/pratham/issues)

---

## What is Pratham?

Pratham is everything a first-time Indian founder needs to begin — in one place, open source, and free forever.

Five layers of resources, all contributed by the community:

| Layer | What it contains |
|---|---|
| 📚 **Learn** | Stage-wise books, videos, podcasts, case studies |
| 🏛️ **Programs** | City-wise incubators, accelerators, and free programs |
| 🤝 **Mentors** | Founders and experts available at nominal cost |
| 💰 **Grants** | Government, institutional, and private funding opportunities |
| 🌱 **Community** | Peer network for student founders and builders |

---

## Why free forever?

Most first-time founders in India start with no roadmap. This knowledge is scattered, paywalled, and gatekept. Pratham changes that.

**Funding model:**
- 1% of every Adhvira product's revenue
- 2% of every Forge project delivery
- Community donations
- Institutional and CSR partnerships

If Adhvira grows, Pratham grows with it.
