# Contributing to Pratham

Thank you for contributing. Every addition helps a founder who doesn't know where to start.

---

## What you can contribute

### 📚 Learn — Learning resources

A book, YouTube video, podcast, or case study that helped you as a founder.

Add to the relevant file in `/learn/` based on the stage it applies to:

- `pre-idea.md` — Finding a problem worth solving
- `idea-stage.md` — Validating your idea
- `validation.md` — Testing with real users
- `building.md` — Building the product
- `scaling.md` — Growing beyond early traction

**Format:**
```markdown
### [Resource Title](link)
**Type:** Book / Video / Podcast / Case Study  
**Author / Channel:** Name  
**Why it helps:** One sentence on what a founder gets from this.
```

---

### 🏛️ Programs — Incubators and programs

An incubator, accelerator, or government program supporting early-stage Indian startups.

Add to the relevant city file in `/programs/` or to `national.md` for pan-India programs.

**Format:**
```markdown
### Program Name
**Organisation:** Name of the institution  
**City:** City / Online  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Cost:** Free / Nominal (₹X) / Paid  
**Duration:** X months  
**What they offer:** 2–3 lines on what founders get  
**Apply:** [Link](url)  
**Deadline:** Month Year / Rolling  
```

---

### 🤝 Mentors — Mentor profiles

A founder or expert willing to offer mentoring at free or nominal cost.

Add to `/mentors/` as a new file: `firstname-lastname.md`

Use the template in `/mentors/mentor-template.md`

> **Note:** Mentor profiles are reviewed by the Pratham team before merging. We verify that the person is real and the profile is accurate.

---

### 💰 Grants — Funding opportunities

A grant, scheme, or funding opportunity for Indian founders.

Add to the relevant file in `/grants/`:
- `government.md` — Central and state government schemes
- `institutions.md` — University and institutional grants
- `private.md` — Private foundation and corporate grants

**Format:**
```markdown
### Grant / Scheme Name
**Source:** Organisation name  
**Amount:** ₹X – ₹Y (or range)  
**Eligibility:** Who can apply (DPIIT registered / student / etc.)  
**Stage:** Idea / Pre-revenue / Early revenue  
**Deadline:** Month Year / Rolling / Annual  
**Apply:** [Link](url)  
**Notes:** Any important conditions or tips.  
**Last verified:** Month Year  
```

---

### 🌱 Community — Links and channels

A WhatsApp group, Discord server, Slack, or forum for Indian founders.

Add to `/community/README.md`

**Format:**
```markdown
### Community Name
**Platform:** WhatsApp / Discord / Slack / Other  
**Focus:** What the community is about  
**Size:** Approximate member count  
**Join:** [Link](url)  
```

---

## How to submit a contribution

1. **Fork** the repository
2. **Create a branch** — `add/resource-name` or `fix/grant-deadline`
3. **Make your changes** following the formats above
4. **Open a pull request** with a clear title and a one-line description of what you added
5. **Wait for review** — we aim to review PRs within 3 days

---

## What makes a good contribution

✅ Real — the resource, program, or grant actually exists  
✅ Relevant — it applies to Indian founders specifically  
✅ Current — deadlines and links are up to date  
✅ Honest — you've personally used it or can verify it  

❌ Promotional — don't add resources to promote your own product or service  
❌ Duplicate — check if it already exists before adding  
❌ Outdated — don't add programs that are no longer active  

---

## Updating existing entries

Found a broken link? A grant deadline that's passed? A program that's closed?

Open a PR fixing it, or [open an issue](https://github.com/adhvira/pratham/issues) flagging it. Both are equally valuable.
