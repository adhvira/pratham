# Mentors

Founders and experts willing to provide mentoring to first-time Indian founders at free or nominal cost.

All profiles are self-submitted and reviewed by the Pratham team before going live.

---

## How to submit your profile

1. Copy `mentor-template.md`
2. Create a new file: `firstname-lastname.md`
3. Fill in all fields honestly
4. Open a pull request

We review and merge mentor profiles within 5 business days.

---

## What makes a good mentor profile

- You have founded, built, or operated something real
- You are willing to give at least 1–2 hours per month
- You are honest about your availability and what you can help with
- Your cost is free or genuinely nominal (₹0–₹500 per session)

We do not list paid coaching services. Pratham mentors are here to give back.
